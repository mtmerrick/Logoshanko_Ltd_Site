About Us

Logoshanko Logic is a small software development studio from California, founded in 2020.

Our company goals are to develop software that can help as many people as possible find a job that works for them, at a fair wage!

If you are interested in contributing to this project, visit our crowdfunding page.

We also have a Twitter and a Redbubble Store!

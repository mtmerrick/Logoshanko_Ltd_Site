Industry Index

Industry Index is an extension for Chromium Web Browsers, that integrates with Indeed.

This extension rates companies and job positions based on their accuracy.

Development plans include rating of whether or not listed job 'requirements' based both on common hiring practices for the position, and internal hiring practices for specific companies.

To apply as a tester, please use the Contact Us form.
